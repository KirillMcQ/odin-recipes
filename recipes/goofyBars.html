Goofy Bars

Description

IDK I just googled Goofy recipes and this came up so yeah

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 18 1/4 ounces yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. put eggs in a measuring cup.and beat well.add enough water to make 2/3 cup --
  2. mix well.add cake mix and brown sugar and combine well.
  3. place in a greased 13x9" baking pan -- sprinkle with marshmallows.and chocolate chips --
  4. bake in a 3508 oven for 25-30 minutes.
  5. cool on rack before cutting into bars.
